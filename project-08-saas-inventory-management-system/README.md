# 🚀 Project 08 – SaaS Inventory Management System

## Overview

This project demonstrates a SaaS Inventory Management System developed using Java.

Inventory systems are essential for companies that manage products, stock levels, suppliers, warehouses, purchase requests, and operational availability.

This project simulates a SaaS platform similar to Zoho Inventory, Oracle NetSuite, Odoo Inventory, and SAP Business One.

---

# Business Scenario

A company manages multiple products and needs to track:

- Product records
- Stock quantity
- Low stock alerts
- Supplier details
- Stock-in transactions
- Stock-out transactions
- Inventory reports

The objective is to improve stock visibility and reduce operational shortages.

---

# Objectives

- Add products
- Track stock levels
- Record stock movement
- Detect low stock
- Manage suppliers
- Generate inventory reports
- Support warehouse operations

---

# Key Features

## Product Management

- Add products
- View product inventory
- Track product category
- Monitor product quantity

## Stock Management

- Increase stock
- Reduce stock
- Track stock transactions
- Identify low-stock items

## Supplier Management

- Store supplier details
- Link suppliers to products

## Reporting

- Total products
- Total stock value
- Low-stock products
- Inventory summary
