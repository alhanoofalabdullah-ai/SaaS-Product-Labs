# 🚀 Project 04 – SaaS Customer Relationship Management (CRM) System

## Overview

This project demonstrates a SaaS Customer Relationship Management (CRM) platform developed using Java.

CRM systems are among the most widely used SaaS products in modern organizations because they help companies manage customer interactions, sales pipelines, leads, opportunities, and business relationships.

This project simulates how organizations track prospects from initial lead generation through customer conversion and long-term account management.

---

# Business Scenario

A technology company receives hundreds of leads every month through:

- Website registrations
- Marketing campaigns
- LinkedIn outreach
- Email campaigns
- Referrals

The company requires a centralized CRM platform to manage customer relationships and monitor sales performance.

Sales teams need the ability to:

- Register leads
- Track opportunities
- Convert leads into customers
- Record customer interactions
- Generate sales reports
- Monitor pipeline performance
