# 🚀 Enterprise Fleet Operations & Vehicle Tracking Platform

## Overview

Enterprise Fleet Operations Platform is a SaaS solution designed to manage vehicles, drivers, trips, fuel consumption, maintenance schedules, utilization rates, operational costs, compliance requirements, and fleet performance analytics.

The platform provides centralized visibility over transportation operations and supports data-driven decision making for fleet managers.

This solution simulates enterprise-grade systems used by:

- Logistics Companies
- Transportation Providers
- Government Agencies
- Construction Contractors
- Utility Companies
- Oil & Gas Organizations

---

## Business Problem

Organizations operating large vehicle fleets often face challenges such as:

- Vehicle downtime
- Unplanned maintenance
- High fuel costs
- Driver utilization issues
- Poor operational visibility
- Compliance tracking difficulties

The objective of this platform is to centralize fleet operations and improve operational efficiency.
