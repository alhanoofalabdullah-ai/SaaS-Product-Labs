# 🚀 Project 13 – SaaS Customer Feedback & Survey Management System

## Overview

This project demonstrates a SaaS Customer Feedback and Survey Management System built using Java.

Organizations use survey and feedback platforms to collect customer opinions, measure satisfaction, analyze service quality, identify improvement opportunities, and support strategic decision-making.

This project simulates a professional SaaS platform similar to SurveyMonkey, Qualtrics, Typeform, Google Forms, and Medallia.

---

# Business Scenario

A company serves thousands of customers across multiple products and services.

Management wants to understand:

- Customer satisfaction
- Product quality
- Service performance
- Customer expectations
- Improvement opportunities

The company requires a centralized platform that can create surveys, collect responses, analyze results, and generate customer experience reports.
